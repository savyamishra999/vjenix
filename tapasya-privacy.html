📋 Introduction

TAPASYA is developed and operated by Vjenix Futuristic Technologies, registered under MSME (UDYAM-DL-11-0126075), New Delhi, India.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the TAPASYA mobile application.

By using TAPASYA, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TAPASYA:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address obtained through Google Sign-In
  • Study Data: Syllabus progress, chapter completion status, study session duration, flashcards, and notes you create
  • Usage Data: App features you use, study streaks, XP points, and level progress
  • Payment Information: Transaction details for Divyastra Gold subscriptions (processed securely by Razorpay — we do not store card details)
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app compatibility
  • Profile Picture: If you choose to upload one (stored securely on Supabase)

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the TAPASYA app experience
  • To track and display your study progress, streaks, and achievements
  • To sync your data across devices through secure cloud storage
  • To send daily study reminders (only if you enable them)
  • To process payments for Divyastra Gold subscriptions
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve app features based on anonymous usage analytics

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption.

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
  • Passwords are never stored — we use Google OAuth for authentication
  • Payment processing is handled by Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

🤝 Third-Party Services

TAPASYA uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For secure authentication — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: For secure cloud database and file storage
  • Razorpay: For payment processing — governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy
  • Expo/React Native: For app infrastructure and push notifications

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties.

👶 Children's Privacy

TAPASYA is designed for students aged 13 and above preparing for JEE, NEET, and UPSC examinations.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

⚖️ Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information through the Profile section
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Notifications: Opt out of push notifications anytime in Settings
  • Subscription: Cancel Divyastra Gold subscription anytime
  • Data Portability: Request export of your study data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tapasyatracker@gmail.com

TAPASYA may send push notifications for:

  • Important app updates

You can disable notifications anytime in your device Settings or within the TAPASYA app under Profile → Settings → Daily Reminder.
